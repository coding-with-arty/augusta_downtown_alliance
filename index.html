Our Mission

Our Mission Augusta Downtown Alliance will develop a thriving authentic community. A focal point for life and entertainment in central Maine, by fostering and improving the quality of life, promoting downtown and beautifying our historic city center as a source of pride for residents and visitors alike.

Our Vision

Augusta Downtown Alliance is an exciting and unique downtown that celebrates a thriving authentic community. It is a focal point for life and entertainment in central Maine.

Who We Are

As a largely volunteer organization composed of merchants, developers, and stakeholders, the Augusta Downtown Alliance (ADA) is thrilled to represent this diverse community, and remains committed to further developing it by fostering business growth, improving the region's quality of life, and promoting beautification.
